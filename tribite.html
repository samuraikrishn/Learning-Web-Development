My Inspirations

Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam – Missile Man of India

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam came from humble beginnings, faced many failures, but rose to become the "Missile Man of India" and the People’s President.

"Dream is not that which you see while sleeping, it is something that does not let you sleep."

His journey reminds me that persistence, humility, and vision are the true building blocks of greatness.

Mark Zuckerberg – The Builder of Connections

Mark Zuckerberg inspires me because:

  • Started Young: At 19, he built Facebook from his dorm room without waiting for funding or approval.
  • Focused Vision: His mission was not just a website but to connect the world.
  • Relentless Execution: He kept building even when people didn’t believe in his idea.

This inspires me to start small but start now — whether it’s with my projects or bigger missions like Nestora.
